## 1. Light/Dark Toggle
**Inspiration**: The user's profile image as a manifestation of the theme.
**Description**: Toggles the website between Light and Dark mode.
**How to Trigger**:
- Double-click the **Profile Image** in the Hero section.

## 2. Creative Mode
**Inspiration**: The duality of the user's identityâ€”Logic & Art.
**Description**: Unveils a whimsical, artistic layer over the logical structure of the site, complete with burn effects and new themes.
**How to Trigger**:
- Go to the **About** section.
- Find the text "Logic & Art" (or "Art & Logic").
- **Double-click** it.

## 3. Zoltar Speaks
**Inspiration**: The vintage fortune-telling machine from the 1988 movie *Big*.
**Description**: Summon the mystical Zoltar to grant your wish (or dispense a ticket).
**How to Trigger**:
- Navigate to the **Projects** section.
- Locate the **Zoltar** project card.
- "Rub" the title (move your mouse back and forth over the text "Zoltar") vigorously. You need about 10 back-and-forth motions to wake him up.

## 4. Game Mode
**Inspiration**: Retro gaming consoles, the Konami Code, and classic arcade games.
**Description**: Unlocks a hidden gaming interface where you can choose between multiple games like **Block Blast** and **Snake**.
**How to Trigger**:
1.  **Find the Controller**: Scroll to the **CTA / Principles** section ("Principles for Action"). Click and **hold** (long press) anywhere in that section for **5 seconds** to reveal a D-Pad.
2.  **Unlock the Games**: Use the D-Pad to enter the Konami Code: **Up, Up, Down, Down, Left, Right, Left, Right**.
3.  **Select a Game**: A menu will appear allowing you to play **Block Blast** or **Snake**.

## 5. Everybody to the Limit (Fhqwhgads)
**Inspiration**: Strong Bad's hit song "Everybody to the Limit" from *Homestar Runner*.
**Description**: A full-screen overlay playing the classic flash animation (via Ruffle).
**How to Trigger**:
- Type `fhqwhgads` anywhere on the site.

## 6. IRC Mode (Secret Chat)
**Inspiration**: The "get in touch" form as a gateway to real-time communication.
**Description**: Unlocks a real-time chat interface disguised within the contact form. Use URL params `?chat_id=...` for direct access.
**How to Trigger**:
- Go to the **Get in Touch** form.
- In the **Name** field, append the secret suffix (e.g. ` --irc`).
- Submit the form.
